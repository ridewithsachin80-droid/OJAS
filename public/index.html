📧 Email (Gmail/SMTP)
EMAIL_USER=you@gmail.com
EMAIL_PASS=app_password
EMAIL_FROM_NAME=InvestTrack
EMAIL_HOST=smtp.gmail.com
EMAIL_PORT=587

For Gmail: Enable 2FA → Generate App Password at myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

💬 WhatsApp (Twilio)
TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=ACxxx
TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=xxx
TWILIO_WHATSAPP_FROM=whatsapp:+14155238886
APP_URL=https://your.railway.app

Get Twilio SID & Token at console.twilio.com. Use sandbox number for testing.
